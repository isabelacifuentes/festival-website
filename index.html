About the Event

ELEV8 Festival brings a one of a kind experience of house music, designed to bring people together in an unforgettable elevated atmosphere. With incredible beats, light shows, and a lineup of talented DJs, ELEV8 creates a space where young adults and house music lovers experience more than a party. The music unites everyone bringing the night vibes higher.
